Resources

Anything which can satisfy the need of human is called resources.

Utility of resources gives it a value.

Classification of resources

  1. Natural resources:- Resources that are obtained from nature is called natural resources.
    Ex= Air, water and soil etc.

  2. Human-made resources:- Human modify natural resources and give it a value.
    Ex= Table, chair and desk etc.

  3. Human resources:- Human modify natural resources into Human-made resources. Hence, it is also a resource.


There are two types of natural resources.


  1. Renewablw resources:- Renewable resources are those resources which can be used over and over again by recycle and reuse.
    Ex= Water, soil and bio gas etc.

  2. Non-renewable resources:- Non-renewable resources are the resources which can be used once and after which they can't be used or replaced easily.
    Ex= Oil, coal, and natural gas etc.

Nature of resources

  • Resources are dynamic.

  • Resources are unevenly distributed.

  • Human improve quality and form of natural resources to make it more valuable.

Sustainable Development

  • Development:- Positive growth in the life of humans is called development.

  • Sustainable development:- Developing without harming the nature and taking care of future generation is called sustainable development.

Conservation of Resources

  • Resources are limited and unevenly distributed so, we need to conserve or judicious use of resources. which may include:-

    • 3R-
      • Reduce=> Use minimum resources.
      • Reuse=> Use a resource again and again.
      • Recycle=> Some resources like iron can recycled to new

  • Human is termed as human resource, if th human has:-

    1. A good health

    2. Education

    3. Training or skills


  • Population of the world has increased twice and thrice in recent hundred years whereas, we have limited natural resources.

  • this leads sortage of resource so, we need to manage the resource or judicious use. This is called conservation of resource/li>
