Answer of the Questions.



  1. Data is the raw form of information. Data science refers to the process of converting data into information.

  2. Data science answers following types of questions:-

    1. Yes/No, True/False questions

    2. Anomaly detecting questions

    3. Prediction or forecasts

    4. Grouping


  3. classification refers to the grouping of items or data on the basis of some criteria
    If the grouping is done on the basis of similar properties, then it is called clustering
    Example of classification: Grouping of people based on their age difference.
    Example of clustering: Grouping of people on the basis of their neighbouring state.

  4. Data science can be applied on the following factors:-

    1. Social media

    2. Health care

    3. Banking and finance

    4. E-Cmmerce

    5. SEO
